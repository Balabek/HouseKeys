Unlocking Affordable Housing Opportunities in Local Communities

Homebuilder wants to build a new construction project containing new homes for rent or sale at market rates.

Local Government Agency has requirements or incentives that make a percentage of those homes affordable at below market rates.

HouseKeys facilitates the program for the local government agency, lists the homes for the homebuilder, and assists housing applicants.

What’s different about affordable housing in the SLED Market?

The lack of housing has led to state, local, and education organizations (SLED) developing their own affordable housing programs. This is where HouseKeys helps by aiding these entities and the homebuilders subject to their land use policies.
